# DL-26 — Explore as Primary Post-Auth Topology Surface

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
`/explore` is designated as the primary post-auth topology surface for authenticated users without an observatory. The MVP implementation uses an SVG-based topology (RA center + 7 domain nodes) and deprecates ISSUE-08 PNG-node + mini-map direction as canonical topology presentation.

## Why
- The original PNG-node + mini-map direction did not meet target product quality.
- Explore required a clearer intelligence topology surface for discovery and post-auth orientation.
- SVG topology provides a stable MVP foundation without forcing premature runtime complexity.

## Trade-off
- Gains clearer and more premium MVP topology clarity.
- Defers advanced 3D/engine direction (e.g., Three.js/R3F) to future reconsideration.

## Revisit
Yes. Revisit when observatory-node expansion and advanced graph rendering requirements are prioritized.
