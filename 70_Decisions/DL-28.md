# DL-28 — TopBar Canonical Roles and Routing Discipline

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
TopBar follows canonical role-based navigation rules:
- Logo routes to `/`.
- guest → About / Log in / Get Started
- authNoObservatory → Explore / About / Sign out
- authWithObservatory → Explore / Dashboard / Sign out

`Create Observatory` is not a TopBar item; it remains a primary CTA in Explore info panel for eligible users.

## Why
- Prevent duplicate primary CTA behavior between Explore surface and TopBar.
- Keep TopBar focused on navigation and session actions.
- Preserve `/explore` as canonical topology discovery/post-auth surface.

## Trade-off
- Reduces immediate global CTA prominence in header.
- Improves role clarity and avoids conflicting navigation priorities.

## Revisit
Yes. Revisit when dashboard/create flows mature and global nav information architecture changes.
