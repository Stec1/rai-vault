# Terminology Dictionary

## Core Product Terms
- **RA:** The platform identity — the central node of the RAi intelligence topology from which all Domains radiate. Not a user-created object, not a Reputation Asset.
- **Domain:** A logical boundary grouping related systems, observatories, and outputs.
- **Observatory:** The primary workspace for monitoring and presenting AI system context.
- **ObservatoryType:** The observatory classification enum: `individual`, `studio`, or `product`.
- **System:** A modeled AI service, agent, workflow, or tool tracked by RAi.
- **Publication:** A structured output/report created from observatory insights.
- **PublicationUpvote:** A positive feedback event attached to a publication as a lightweight quality signal.
- **Visual Signature:** The brand-consistent presentation profile for outputs.
- **Reputation Score:** A computed trust indicator derived from observable quality and engagement signals.
- **Credit:** A usage unit consumed by product actions and allocations.
- **PlanTier:** The subscription level enum: `free` or `pro`.

## Execution Terms
- **Issue Track**: A scoped implementation step under `40_Execution`.
- **Frozen Context**: Captured assumptions and constraints at execution start.
- **Readiness**: Explicit state proving an issue can begin safely.
