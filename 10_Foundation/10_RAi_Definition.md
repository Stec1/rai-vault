# RAi Definition

RAi is a **premium observatory platform for AI systems**.

## Mission
Provide organizations and advanced builders with structured visibility into AI systems through clear models, observable system state, and publication-grade outputs.

## Product Identity
- Observatory-first language.
- System-level visibility over feature sprawl.
- Professional visual and editorial standards.

## Scope of this definition
This note anchors product language and keeps strategic direction aligned across execution and prompts.
