# RAi: What It Is and Is Not

## RAi Is
- An observatory platform for AI systems.
- A structured layer for domains, systems, observations, and publications.
- A premium product with quality and trust as core value.

## RAi Is Not
- Not a metaverse or spatial-identity product.
- Not a stars/planets/satellites themed product language.
- Not a generic dashboard factory.

## Language Guardrails
Prefer: observatory, system, domain, publication, visual signature.
Avoid: celestial metaphor terminology in active product language.
