# Domain Model

Domains are thematic hubs that give structure to the RAi world map and keep discovery coherent in MVP.
They are not user-owned spaces; they are platform-defined context layers that anchor Observatories and content.

## In MVP
- Total domains are fixed at 7.
- Active domains are:
  - Nexum
  - Keth
  - Solum
- Coming Soon domains are:
  - Vorda
  - Lyren
  - Auren
  - Draxis
- Each Observatory can associate with 1-2 Domains.
- Domains keep fixed positions around RA in the world topology.
- Explore supports thematic filtering by Domain.
- Domain assignment is part of platform information architecture, not free-form tagging.

## Not in MVP
- Enterable 3D/domain spaces.
- User-created Domains.
- User editing of Domain catalog or topology.

## Key constraints
- Domain list and topology are managed centrally by the product team.
- Domain set should remain stable through MVP to avoid discovery fragmentation.
- Any Domain expansion beyond 7 requires formal scope/decision update.
