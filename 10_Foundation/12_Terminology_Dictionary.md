# Terminology Dictionary

## Core Terms
- **Observatory**: The primary workspace for monitoring and presenting AI system context.
- **System**: A modeled AI service or workflow tracked by RAi.
- **Domain**: A logical boundary grouping related systems and outputs.
- **Publication**: A structured output/report created from observatory insights.
- **Visual Signature**: The brand-consistent presentation profile for outputs.

## Execution Terms
- **Issue Track**: A scoped implementation step under `40_Execution`.
- **Frozen Context**: Captured assumptions and constraints at execution start.
- **Readiness**: Explicit state proving an issue can begin safely.
