# DL-22: Reputation Score, Not Star Ratings

> Vault-side mirror of repo decision DL-22 per [[DL-007]] lazy mirror policy.
> Authoritative source: `docs/decision-log.md` in the RAi product repo.

## Status
Accepted (mirrored)

## Decision
Observatory reputation is a numeric score accumulated from activity and community evaluation — not a star rating or review average.

## Formula
- Complete Observatory profile: +10
- Register System: +5
- Publish research: +5
- Publication reaches 10+ upvotes: +10
- Publication reaches 50+ upvotes: +25
- Consistent publishing (4+ per month): +15 streak bonus

## Rationale (summary)
Accumulated score rewards consistent quality over time; star ratings encourage gaming and create perception problems; score-based ranking in Explore creates clear incentive to publish and improve.

## Revisit condition
Formula may need tuning after soft launch based on actual distribution — tracked in `docs/backlog.md`.
